title: Best practices
description: How to get the most out of PhysiClaw — treat it as an assistant that learns you over time, be clear about what you want, and keep coaching it as you go.
---

PhysiClaw is your personal assistant. A few small habits go a long way toward making it work well for you.

## Treat it like an assistant, not a product

PhysiClaw gets to know your preferences as you go. Your feedback is what it improves on — it keeps getting better the more you correct it.

## Be specific about what you want

The clearer your request, the fewer wrong turns it takes. In particular, name the app you want it to use.

:::tip[Message]
Order a half-gallon of Horizon organic 2% milk on Instacart.
:::

## Use "remember" to improve it

Anything you want it to hold onto for the long run — your preferences, a saved address, your rules — just tell it to remember, and it'll carry that into every future conversation. Corrections work the same way: point out what went wrong, have it remember, and it won't repeat the mistake.

:::tip[Message]
Remember: I don't like spicy food.
:::

## Let it take a few tries

Some things that are effortless for you can trip it up — an unexpected pop-up, a screen it hasn't seen before. Most of the time it'll retry and figure it out, but now and then it'll get stuck and ask for your help.

## Schedule recurring tasks

PhysiClaw can run tasks on a schedule — just tell it when.

:::tip[Message]
Every morning at 8:00, tell me the weather.
:::
